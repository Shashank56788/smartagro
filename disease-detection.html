How Disease Detection Works

Advanced computer vision powered by deep learning

1

Image Capture

Take a clear photo of the affected plant part (leaf, fruit, or stem) using your smartphone or camera. Ensure good lighting and focus on the diseased area.

Tips:
  • ✓ Use natural lighting
  • ✓ Fill the frame with the affected area
  • ✓ Keep camera steady and focused
  • ✓ Avoid shadows and glare
2

Image Preprocessing

The AI system automatically preprocesses your image by resizing, normalizing pixel values, and enhancing features relevant to disease identification.

Image Normalization Feature Enhancement Background Removal
3

CNN Analysis

A Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on thousands of plant disease images analyzes patterns, colors, spots, and textures to identify the disease.

Deep Learning Pattern Recognition Multi-class Classification
4

Disease Identification

The system provides the disease name, confidence score, severity level, treatment recommendations, and preventive measures.

95%+ Accuracy 40+ Diseases Instant Results
